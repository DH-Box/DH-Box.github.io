About


DH Box is a Linux computer in the cloud. It contains popular Digital Humanities inquiry software- pre-installed and configured so users can move straight to exploring powerful computational tools immediately, even if they lack deep computational literacy or full service IT support. DH Box is accessible directly from your web browser - all you need is an internet connection.

Teachers can deploy a computer lab in the cloud, eliminating the need to spend hours fiddling with a roomful of computers. Use the computational tools in DH Box to work collaboratively with students on asking questions, finding answers, and reviewing work.

Students can begin using DH tools to develop concrete, widely applicable computational skills without having to purchase equipment or download software. At the same time, they can produce meaningful and easily transmitted works of scholarship.


If you are a teacher, scholar, or student interested in taking a 'DH' approach to things, DH Box is the perfect place to start.
